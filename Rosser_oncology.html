Image by Airman 1st Class Bailee Darbasie via nellis.af.mil

Cancer is defined by the U.S. Centers for Disease Control as a disease of abnormal cells dividing without control that spreads and invades other tissues through blood and lymph systems. Genetic changes or hazardous environments can lead to damage of DNA. There are many different types of cancer, please look into the CDC's list of kinds of cancer.

LPG's Oncology Clinic has leading doctors and staff in the field for recognizing and diagnosing appropriate treatments for patients. Ensuring flexible care according to the patient and familys' needs. The clinic has an on-site labratory for prevention and treatment with PET/CT image scanning for prompt accurate diagnosis.
