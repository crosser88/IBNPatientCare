Cardiology


Image by Nellis AFB, Nev. via nellis.af.mil

According to the U.S. Centers for Disease Control, heart disease causes 1 in 4 deaths. Help prevent cardiovascular complications by keeping up with a wellness checkup, eating fresh fruits and vegetables, quit smoking, regular exercise, and maintaining a healthy BMI (Body Mass Index).

LPG's highly experienced physicians and nursing staff treat numerous emergency or non-emergency heart conditions. LPG's cardiologists and the 24-hour-a-day emergency department will ensure you get the cardio care you need. Some of the services provided:

  • Stress test - evaluation on the heart's performance during physical activities.
  • Electrocardiogram (EKG) - measures the heart's electrical activity.
  • Heart valve pressure - tests to provide doctor's information about the condition of heart valves.
  • Ventricular function tests - heart's ability to pump blood.
  • Coronary angiography - evaluates arteries, locates blockages and diagnostics for heart disease.
