Wellness Checkup


Image by Airman Zachary Rufus via nellis.af.mil

Wellness checkups are performed every six months or the next time of visit. Patients are notified when approaching their due checkup. Checkups track the patient's blood pressure, heart rate, weight, height, and list of medication(s). This allows the primary care physician of the patient to analyze the data and recognize any possible health complications.
